Frequently Asked Questions

How do I borrow a digital book?

+

To borrow a digital book, simply log in to your account, search for the book you want, and click the "Borrow" button. The book will be added to your digital library for the specified loan period.

Can I read books offline?

+

Yes, you can download books for offline reading using our mobile app. This allows you to enjoy your borrowed books even without an internet connection.

How long can I keep a borrowed book?

+

The standard loan period is 14 days, but this may vary depending on the book and your membership level. You can check the exact loan period for each book in your digital library.
